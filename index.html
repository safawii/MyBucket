How it works




Create a list

Want to go camping in the Antarctic? Go Ostrich Riding? Or get a tattoo? Compile a list of all the things you want to do atleast once. Get suggestions according to your location of places where you can complete your desired experience.

Share Experiences

Spotted someone with the same interests as you? Well, you can approach them and go with them for one of your experiences together. It is a great way to get to know people!

Sponsor your friends

Have a friend who's birthday is coming up and don't know what to get them? Don't worry about spending lots of money on a gift you're not even sure they'll like. You can simply sponsor one of the items on your friend's bucket list.
